# Products-Demand-Availability

📊 Product Performance Insights: Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline

📦 Product Performance Insights: Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyse product-level performance, uncover sales trends, and evaluate profitability across categories, regions, and time periods.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Product Performance Insights Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report built to help users explore sales, profit, and product trends. It enables businesses to identify top-performing products, monitor revenue patterns, and make data-driven decisions to optimise product strategy.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Primary platform for building interactive dashboards
📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created measures for KPIs, profit calculations, and trend analysis
📝 Data Modelling – Relationships between sales, products, and customer datasets to enable cross-filtering
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and dashboard delivery

4. Data Source

Source: Product sales dataset (retail/e-commerce transactional data)

The dataset includes:

Product details (category, sub-category, product name)
Sales metrics (revenue, quantity sold, discounts)
Profit and cost-related measures
Customer and regional data
Time-based fields for trend analysis

This structured dataset enables multidimensional analysis of product performance and business outcomes.

5. Features / Highlights
   
• Business Problem
Organisations often struggle to gain clear visibility into product performance due to scattered or complex datasets. Without an intuitive dashboard, it becomes difficult to:

Identify top and underperforming products
Track profitability across categories
Understand sales trends over time
Make strategic inventory and pricing decisions

Key questions include:

Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
Which categories are underperforming?
How do sales and profit trends change over time?
What regions or segments contribute most to revenue?
• Goal of the Dashboard

To deliver an interactive analytics tool that:

Provides a clear overview of sales and profitability
Enables deep-dive analysis at product and category levels
Supports strategic decision-making in pricing, inventory, and marketing
Identifies growth opportunities and performance gaps
• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

🔹 Key KPIs (Top Section)

Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin (%)
Total Quantity Sold
Number of Products

🔹 Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
Displays revenue distribution across product categories, helping identify high-performing segments.

🔹 Profit by Sub-Category (Bar / Column Chart)
Highlights which sub-categories contribute most to profitability.

🔹 Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
Shows sales performance across months/years to identify seasonality and growth patterns.

🔹 Top Products (Table / Bar Chart)
Ranks products based on sales or profit contribution.

🔹 Region-wise Performance (Map / Bar Chart)
Analyses geographic contribution to overall sales and profit.

🔹 Discount Impact Analysis (Scatter / Column Chart)
Explores how discounts affect sales volume and profitability.

🔹 Filters / Slicers Panel
Interactive filters for:

Category / Sub-category
Region
Time period
Product selection

• Business Impact & Insights
📈 Revenue Optimisation: Identify best-selling products and focus on high-performing categories
💰 Profitability Analysis: Detect low-margin products and improve pricing strategies
🎯 Targeted Decision-Making: Enable category-wise and region-wise strategy planning
📦 Inventory Planning: Optimise stock levels based on product demand trends
📊 Trend Analysis: Understand seasonality and forecast future sales
